Our story

On 21st March 2016, 3 young talents met each other in a cafe in LA after helping their families to pack their daily necessities from Hong Kong, Melbourne and London respectively. They were complaining about the drawbacks of counterfeit products while drinking coffee.

3 months later, an ICO which aimed to fight against counterfeit products was started. They had raised 5 million USD during the ICO. However, when the investors urged them to get listed on a major exchange platform in order to expand the size of business a month later, 2 million USD was charged by the platform. After 2 months, the crypto company resulted in the end of operation and huge debt.

An excellent concept was obstructed and obliterated by the exchange platform which wants a huge profit. This is just the tip of the iceberg. This is what Minus Group see when helping the startups in many years. Creativity, technology and fund are three essential elements for inventions which lead to improvement of human being. An exchange platform is the cradle of creative concepts. But when does it become a terminator of those great projects? Our aim is to build a community and create mutual prosperity with ICOs.
